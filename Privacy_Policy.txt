Privacy Policy for Luid

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Luid (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information. Luid is a water and drink tracking app designed to help users log fluid intake, review hydration-related habits, set reminders, view local insights, and optionally save water entries to Apple Health.

Luid is designed with privacy in mind. Most app information is stored locally on your device. Luid does not sell your data. Luid does not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, third-party crash analytics SDKs, data brokers, cross-app tracking, or a custom server-based account system.

Some features rely on Apple system services, such as Apple Health, StoreKit/App Store purchases, local notifications, selected photo access, Apple Watch connectivity, Apple diagnostics, and device or iCloud backup settings controlled by Apple. Apple may process information according to Apple’s own privacy practices, system settings, and user choices.

2. Data the App may process

Luid may process the following data depending on the features you use.

2.1 Profile and onboarding data

The App may store:

* First name
* Last name
* Age
* Weight
* Sex/gender selection
* Activity level
* Preferred measurement unit
* Optional profile image
* Onboarding completion status
* App settings

This information is used to personalize your local tracking experience, calculate a conservative wellness tracking estimate, display your profile, and configure the App.

Age, weight, sex/gender, activity level, and related profile inputs are used only for wellness tracking personalization inside the App. They are not used for medical diagnosis, treatment, prevention, cure, dosage guidance, or emergency decisions.

2.2 Drink and hydration data

The App may store:

* Drink entries
* Drink amount
* Drink type, such as water, coffee, tea, or supported drinks
* Custom drink name, color, and related custom drink settings
* Date and time of entries
* Entry creation date
* Whether an entry counts toward your daily total
* Daily goal
* Quick-add amounts
* Default drink type
* Daily context settings
* Weekly and historical summaries generated from your entries

This data is used to provide water and drink tracking, progress views, history, charts, summaries, local insights, and related App features.

2.3 Reminder data

If you use reminders, the App may store:

* Hydration reminder settings
* Reminder schedule mode
* Fixed reminder times
* Interval reminder settings
* Repeat weekdays
* Quiet hours
* Goal-complete pause setting
* Smart or adaptive reminder settings
* Custom reminder titles
* Custom reminder notes
* Custom reminder times
* Reminder enabled/disabled status

Luid uses iOS local notifications for reminders. The App does not use push notification servers and does not upload notification tokens to any server.

Local notification delivery depends on iOS, device settings, Focus modes, notification permissions, battery state, system scheduling, and other conditions controlled by the device or Apple. The developer cannot guarantee that every notification will be delivered, delivered at an exact time, displayed, heard, or acted on.

2.4 Selected photo data

The App may allow you to choose a profile photo using Apple’s selected photo picker.

The App processes only the image you select for the profile photo feature. Luid does not use the camera for profile photos and does not scan your full photo library. Your selected profile image may be stored locally as part of your App profile.

If you later export, share, back up, or otherwise move the image outside Luid through system features or device backups, that activity is controlled by your device settings, Apple services, or the destination you choose.

2.5 Apple Health data

Luid may offer optional Apple Health support.

When Apple Health support is enabled and permission is granted, Luid may save new drink entries to Apple Health as dietary water. Luid requests permission to write water data to Apple Health. The App does not need to read your Apple Health data for this feature.

Apple Health permission is controlled by you through iOS and the Health app. You may grant, deny, or revoke Apple Health permissions using Apple’s system settings.

Turning off Apple Health support in Luid stops new Health writes from Luid where technically available. Deleting or editing Luid entries may attempt to delete or update matching Apple Health water samples created by Luid, where permission and Apple Health availability allow it.

The developer cannot guarantee that Apple Health data will always be saved, updated, deleted, synchronized, displayed, or restored correctly. Apple Health behavior may depend on iOS, Health app settings, device availability, user permissions, iCloud Health settings, Apple ID status, and Apple system behavior.

You remain responsible for reviewing and managing Apple Health data inside the Apple Health app. Luid should not be used as the sole source of truth for health, medical, safety, or emergency information.

2.6 Apple Watch connectivity data

If Luid is used with Apple Watch support, the App may exchange limited hydration-related data between your paired iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple WatchConnectivity.

This may include:

* Today’s hydration total
* Daily goal
* Measurement unit
* Quick-add amounts
* Drink catalog items
* Custom drink identifiers or display information
* Today’s progress segments
* Drink entries added from the Apple Watch

This data is used only to keep the Luid experience consistent between your paired devices. Watch connectivity depends on Apple hardware, watchOS, iOS, pairing state, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi availability, device reachability, Apple system behavior, and user settings.

The developer cannot guarantee uninterrupted sync, instant delivery, complete delivery, or perfect state matching between devices.

2.7 Subscription data

Luid uses Apple StoreKit and the App Store for subscriptions and purchases.

Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, billing, taxes, pricing, payment methods, currency conversion, offer eligibility, trial eligibility, family sharing availability, chargebacks, and financial transaction handling are managed by Apple App Store and StoreKit.

We do not receive, process, or store your payment card information.

The App may check Apple StoreKit transaction and entitlement information to determine whether Luid Pro features should be unlocked. The App may keep a local or in-memory subscription entitlement state as needed to provide Pro access.

The developer does not control Apple’s billing systems, refund decisions, App Store account systems, tax handling, regional pricing, subscription renewal, subscription cancellation, payment authorization, or StoreKit availability.

2.8 Luid Pro and local insight data

Some advanced features may require Luid Pro.

Luid Pro insights, charts, trend analysis, reminder intelligence, drink balance insights, calendar heatmaps, personal records, recap views, custom drink features, and other advanced features are generated from your local App data where available.

Luid does not send your drink history, profile details, reminders, or local insight data to an external analysis server.

Local insights depend on the completeness and accuracy of the information you enter. If entries are missing, incorrect, duplicated, delayed, edited, deleted, or recorded under the wrong drink type, the resulting charts and insights may be incomplete or inaccurate.

10. Delete account, reset, and data deletion

Luid may include local reset or delete flows.

These may delete or reset:

* Local drink entries
* Local hydration history
* Local reminders
* Scheduled Luid local notifications
* Local profile data
* Local profile image
* Local settings
* Local custom drinks
* Local onboarding state

Because Luid does not use a custom backend account system, “Delete Account”, “Reset All Local Data”, or similar wording refers to deleting or resetting the local app profile and related App data on the device where the action is performed.

This may not remove Apple Health data if permission is denied, HealthKit is unavailable, the data cannot be matched, Apple Health fails the deletion request, the sample was changed outside Luid, the device is offline where relevant, or Apple systems do not complete the operation.

You can also manage Apple Health water data directly in the Apple Health app.

Deleting local App data may not delete:

* Apple App Store purchase history
* Apple subscription records
* Apple invoices, billing, tax, or refund records
* Apple diagnostics
* Apple Health data not removable by Luid
* Device or iCloud backups controlled by Apple
* Files you exported or shared outside the App
